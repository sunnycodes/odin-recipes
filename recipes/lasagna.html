Lasagna

Description

A steaming plate of lasagna, with golden, bubbly cheese on top, reveals layers of rich, savory meat sauce, tender pasta, and creamy béchamel. The edges are slightly crispy, while the center remains soft and comforting.

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and ground beef, cooking until the beef is browned and the onion is soft.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer lasagna noodles over the sauce, then top with ricotta mixture, followed by mozzarella cheese and more meat sauce.
  8. Repeat the layering process until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
